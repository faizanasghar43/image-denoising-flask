About DnoiseIt

Artist: Paven

DenoiseIt is a “smart” image denoiser. Utilizing a combination of artificial intelligence and python, the software can denoise image up to fullest.

System

The project operates through software that links to a camera for face detection. Upon recognizing a viewer, ClockWise displays the time of their last visit. For repeat viewers, it shows the last time they checked, creating a unique interaction that plays on human memory and the concept of time perception. New faces trigger the clock to record the current time, storing this data to display upon their return. This inventive approach reimagines the traditional clock, making each glance a personal and reflective experience.
